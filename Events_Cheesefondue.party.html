16.11.2018, 7pm until late 到很晚

Cheese Fondue Craze 奶酪火锅聚会

We got bubbling, delicious news for you which are heart-melting but at the same time also a little crazy. Before it is getting too cheesy, let’s unveil the mystery!

It’s Cheese Fondue Craze @ Zarah!!

Friday November 16th we are kicking off the Cheese Fondue season with a special event where you can live out your secret dreams about bubbling pots of HOT, melted Swiss cheese.
From 7pm onwards our famous Cheese Fondue will be available which will come with a 3 hours all-you-can-eat “Fondue Craze” buffet line to make your dreams come true! So let’s go nuts with a crazy selection of free-flow appetizers such as different kinds of bruschetta & salads, various popular fondue dippers incl. the classics like cornichons, mini pearl onions, baby potatoes, mushrooms etc. But also some Beijing classics will be available for example sweet & purple potatoes, lotus root, broccoli and more. If meat rather than veggies is playing the starring role in your Cheese-Fondue dreams, then don’t worry. Our colorful “Fondue Craze” buffet will also feature some cold cuts and sausages. Oh, and let’s not forget about the homemade desserts to pleasure the sweet stomach after all the craze and neither about the live DJs who will bring you into the party mood until late hours.

Also starting from November 17th every night from 6pm onwards our regular Cheese Fondue will be available at Zarah (RMB 298 net set for two, last order 9pm). But unfortunately without our free-flow “Fondue Craze” buffet.
So, if you finally want to live out your secret Cheese Fondue dreams, then let’s reveal our hidden pleasures together at the “Cheese Fondue Craze” at Zarah!

我们有一个疯狂的能将你融化的好消息要告诉你，在它变的太cheesy之前，让我们揭开谜团吧！

就是在Zarah的奶酪火锅聚会！！

11月16日，周五，我们将有一个特别的活动来开启今年奶酪火锅季，你的瑞士奶酪火锅梦想将会成真。
我们倍受欢迎的奶酪火锅将于晚上7点开始，会有让您梦想成真3个小时的“奶酪聚会”自助餐！所以，让我们疯狂的选择不限量的开胃菜吧，比如不同种类的布如斯开塔和沙拉，还有种类丰富的火锅蘸菜：像经典的酸黄瓜，迷你酸洋葱，小土豆，口蘑等；当然也会有一些经典的北京蘸菜：比如紫薯，莲藕，西兰花等。如果在你的奶酪火锅梦里肉类比蔬菜扮演着更重要的角色，那也不用担心，我们丰富多彩的奶酪火锅也会有冷切和香肠。哦，当然也不要忘了能使您大快朵颐的自制甜点，还有能够使您拥有聚会心情直至深夜的现场DJ们。

另外，11月17日开始，每晚6点开始，我们的奶酪火锅回归啦（298/2位，21:00截止点单）！温馨提示：回归的普通酪火锅不含自助。 所以，如果你想实现自己的秘密奶酪火锅梦，那就让我们一起相聚在Zarah，探寻“奶酪火锅聚会”隐藏的乐趣吧！
