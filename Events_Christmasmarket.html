01.12.2018,11am-4pm

Give a present Christmas Market
赠送礼物圣诞市场

Christmas is around the corner bringing along conflicting feelings: it’s a time to be merry but also a time when mass consumption, not LOVE, rules. One short stroll around any Beijing shopping mall will confirm that it is not LOVE that is all around us, but stuff to buy turning even the most honest Yuletide spirit into mere profits.
We don’t want to sound like a Grinch who is destroying Christmas dreams because we LOVE Christmas. We just don’t like how it has become a holiday of intense materialism, superficial gift-exchanging & feasting and less about family gatherings, hope and giving back.
If you also think it’s time to put an end to the mindless consumerism, then join our “Give a Present Christmas Market” @ Zarah on December 1st.
Get special gifts such as home-made jams, chutneys & peanut butters, self-made jewelry, bags & clothes, organic beauty products and many other special DIY handicrafts and support local businesses at the same time.
If you are advocating a totally gift-free Christmas, then still come and don’t buy any presents but “give” presents to children in need. Also this year we will collect gifts under our beautiful Christmas Tree which will be sent to the Beijing-based NGO “Migrant Children Foundation” (MCF) that aims to help disadvantaged children from migrant workers’ families in Beijing.
Let’s reclaim the beautiful holiday spirit and enjoy some freshly baked Christmas Cookies, German Lebkuchen and homemade waffles, while the lovely scent of roasted almonds and our secret recipe Gluehwine will fill the air.
We will also have a kid’s corner where your little ones can do beautiful handicrafts and other Christmas activities organized by our friends from Kids Planet Hutong. Let’s celebrate kindness, charity and support or simply Christmas in the most sincere way!

圣诞节就要到了，同时也带来了矛盾的感觉：圣诞节本是一个快乐，充满爱的时刻，而现在却演变成了大众消费的日子。
在北京任何一家购物中心闲逛一下，都会不难发现，围绕着我们的不是“爱”，而是可以买的到的东西，这使得最诚实的圣诞老人精神变成了存粹的利润。 我们不想成为听起来像是一个破坏圣诞梦想的扫兴的人，因为我们热爱圣诞节。我们只是不喜欢这个节日变成了物质主义的节日，肤浅的礼物交换和各种聚会，而关于家庭聚会，希望和回报的感觉少之又少。
如果你也认为现在是时候结束盲目的消费主意了，那就来参加我们12月1号的“赠送礼物圣诞市场”吧！
从自制果酱、酸辣酱、花生酱、自制珠宝、小袋子、衣服、有机美容品，还有很多其他DIY的手工艺品中挑选特别礼物的同时，你也支持了本地的小生意。
如果你提倡一个完全无礼物的圣诞节，那么还是可以过来的，不用买集市上的礼物，但是可以“送”礼物给有需要的孩子。同样的，今年我们也将在我们的美丽的圣诞树下收集礼物，这些礼物将被送到北京的非政府组织“流动儿童基金会”（MCF）其目的是帮助北京外来务工家庭的贫困儿童。
让我们重新回到美丽的节日氛围，在弥漫着的烤杏仁和秘密配方热红酒的香气里，享受着新鲜出炉的圣诞饼干、徳式圣诞蛋糕，还有自制松饼。 我们来自Kids Planet Hutong的老朋友也会同往常一样，为小家伙们准备儿童乐园，在那小家伙们可以动手制作漂亮的手工艺品，参加一些其他的圣诞活动。
让我们以最诚挚的方式来庆祝友好的、慈善的、简单的圣诞节！

Merry Christmas! 圣诞快乐！
