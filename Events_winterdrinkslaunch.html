07.12.2018, 7pm until late 直至深夜

Winter drinks launch party
冬季饮品派对

It’s too late, there is no escape anymore … “IT” is here now!
No, we are not talking about Stephen Kings’s horror novel “IT” from 1986, we are talking about Beijing Winter which is here now (and has been here in 1986 too though)! But don’t worry, we already have a plan on how to face “IT” together with "joint powers"!
Come to Zarah this Friday December 7th, join our “Winter Drinks Launch Party” and get your winter power injection! Maybe our new winter drinks won’t make you immortal but happier for sure, while a happy soul boosts the immune system!
Our special Winter Drinks Menu will be available from 7pm onwards with a whole night Happy Hour at RMB 40 for all newcomers! Just to make sure you will get enough power shots to fight “IT”, the merciless Beijing Winter. But that’s not where our Beijing-Winter-Survival-Plan ends! To turn up the resistance to “IT”, we have also launched a new, extended Winter Happy Hour (6pm-9pm) which will additionally kick in during the “Winter Drinks Launch Party”. Besides the extra hour extension, we will also offer all our open wines, Prosecco + Gluehwine at RMB 30 per glass from this Friday onwards.
So come to Zarah this Friday and get your power shot to combat Beiing Winter, while our live DJs will create the ambiance for you until late hours!
