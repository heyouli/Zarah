25.11.2018, 5pm

My Christmas Cookie for YOU
我为你制作圣诞饼干

Also this year we are organizing in partnership with the Beijing-based NGO Migrant Children Foundation (MCF) & F&B Solutions a big Cookie-Baking Party where you can craft cookies, eat, drink, dance & send a self-made Christmas gift to disadvantaged children.
We will start first with the cookie-baking session where you can mix and match different ingredients from our extensive buffet selection to create YOUR-style Christmas cookies. All sorts of decoration supplies will be on hand – everything from dried fruits and nuts to sunflower seeds and M&Ms – so get creative. Please don’t forget to grab your cookie-cutters when you come to Zarah!
While you are waiting for the cookies to bake, we will throw an afterparty featuring a festive Christmas dinner buffet with lots of homemade delicacies and also delightful desserts kindly offered by F&B solutions. Let’s not forget about music: our DJs will entertain you with a live performance and a chance to dance & further socialize with all the other cookie-makers!
Since Christmas is all about SHARING, at the end of the evening one bag of your self-made cookies will be sent to migrant children as Christmas gift!
At last year’s cookie baking event (December 10th, 2017) 150 cookies were crafted and delivered to migrant Children as Christmas gift on December 13th, 2017 by MCF.

同样的，今年我们也正在和设在北京的非政府组织“流动儿童基金会” (MCF)，还有法念食品有限公司筹划一场盛大的烘焙聚会，在聚会上你可以制作不同形状的饼干，吃，喝，跳舞，同时为有需要的孩子们送出自制圣诞礼物。
聚会将从饼干烘焙开始，你可以从水果干，坚果，葵花籽，M&M豆等各种食材中选择搭配混合来制作属于你自己独特风格的圣诞饼干。所以，一定要记得带好饼干模具来Zarah呦！
在等待饼干烘焙的时间呢，我们将会有一个圣诞主题的afterparty，有很多自制的美味佳肴，还有法念食品有限公司提供惹人喜爱的甜点。当然，还有party必不可少的音乐，我们的DJ们用他们的现场演出使你忘却烦恼，并且有机会和其他烘焙聚会参与者一起联欢跳舞直至深夜……
圣诞是分享的季节，所以聚会结束后，将会有一袋你自己制作的饼干作为圣诞礼物送给农民工子女！
2017年12月10日的烘培聚会，我们共做了150块饼干，这些饼干由MCF于2017年12月13日作为圣诞礼物全部送给了农民工子女。
