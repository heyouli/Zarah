From 17.11. every night after 6pm 11月17日起 /每晚6点开始

Cheese Fondue 奶酪火锅

Starting from 17th of November, every night after 6pm our regular Cheese Fondue will be available at Zarah (RMB 298 net set for two, last order 9pm).
For the ones who are giving secretly the blank look (“What the hell is Cheese Fondue?”): Cheese Fondue which was promoted as a Swiss National Dish by the Swiss Cheese Union in the 1930s simply means “dip-bits-of-food-in-a-communal-pot” similar to Chinese hotpot!
However, instead of a spicy Sichuan pepper infused broth, our ceramic pot at Zarah is filled with melted Swiss Gruyere & Emmentaler Cheese with a HINT of “Kirschwasser” and served with long-stemmed forks instead of chopsticks.
Moreover we will offer a variety of delicious dippers such as crunchy cubes of bread, a plate of healthy vegetables, a mixed side of cornichons, pearl onions and cocktail tomatoes as well as a bowl of pineapple to dip into our Swiss bubbling pot!
Beijing winter is approaching! So keep in mind, you are never alone: there is always a bubbling pot waiting for you at Zarah after 6pm!

从11月17号晚开始，每晚6点以后，我们的奶酪火锅回归啦（298/2位，21:00截止点单）！
给那些想了解，又不知道奶酪火锅是什么的人们解释一下：上世纪30年代瑞士奶酪联合会将奶酪火锅提升为瑞士国菜，意思是“在一个共用的锅里“涮”菜，类似于中国的火锅。
当然，我们的陶制火锅里没有四川火锅的麻辣锅底，也没有筷子，而有的是装满了融化了的古老耶奶酪&大孔奶酪，还有少量的樱桃酒，和长把叉子。
此外，奶酪火锅也会有丰富美味的蘸菜：像松脆的面包块，健康蔬菜拼盘，酸黄瓜、酸洋葱、圣女果拼盘，还有一碗菠萝块！
北京的冬天就要到了！但请记着，你永远都不孤单：因为每晚6点后，在Zarah总有一个热腾腾的奶酪火锅等着你！
